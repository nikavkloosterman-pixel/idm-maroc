Background

About the agency.

International Destination Management Maroc is a destination management company dedicated to Morocco. We design and operate complete itineraries — from the first arrival transfer to the final departure — for individual travellers, corporate groups and couples.

The agency operates under NR1 Commercialisation SARL, its registered commercial entity. That structure lets us contract, invoice and partner with the clarity that hotels, venues, transport providers and tour operators expect.

Our focus is narrow on purpose: one country, covered properly. From Tangier and Chefchaouen in the north to Marrakech, the Atlas and the Saharan south, we plan around the realities of each region — distances, seasons, and the local partners who do the work well.

Our aim is simple: itineraries that travellers remember, and partnerships that local businesses can count on.
